Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

How is this different from a traditional lead gen agency? +
Most agencies charge a flat monthly retainer — you pay whether you get results or not. We don't work that way. You pay a one-time infrastructure setup fee, then per qualified meeting delivered. If we don't perform, we don't get paid. That alignment changes everything about how we operate.
What counts as a "qualified" meeting? +
We define qualified upfront — together. Typically it means a decision-maker from a company that matches your ICP, with a genuine interest in what you offer. We manually vet every reply before booking anything to your calendar. You don't waste time on tire-kickers.
How long before I start seeing results? +
The first 14–21 days are infrastructure and warm-up — we're setting up premium domains and inboxes so your emails actually land. After that, campaigns go live and most clients see their first booked meetings within the first week of launch. The guarantee kicks in at 30 days.
Do I need to write the emails or be involved in the day-to-day? +
Not at all. We handle everything — copywriting, list building, infrastructure, sending, and reply management. Your only job is to show up to the calls we book for you. We do ask for a solid onboarding session upfront so we can nail your ICP and offer positioning.
What industries do you work best with? +
We specialize in staffing firms, manufacturing companies, and B2B service providers doing $1M–$5M/year. These are businesses with a proven offer who need a reliable outbound engine — not startups still figuring out product-market fit.
